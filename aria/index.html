Aria

Lightweight music player for iOS

Aria is a native SwiftUI music player for your local music collection. Designed for lossless audio playback with a clean, minimal interface. No tracking, no ads, just your music.

Audio Quality

Full support for lossless codecs including FLAC, ALAC, and WAV. Enjoy your music in its original quality without any compression artifacts.

Supported Formats

Plays all common audio codecs: FLAC, ALAC, Opus, Vorbis, AAC, MP3, WAV, AIFF, WMA, DSD, APE, and more. Import your music through the Files app with full format compatibility.

Metadata & Lyrics

Complete metadata support displays album art, title, artist, composer, album, and detailed audio information. Embedded lyrics are supported along with synced .lrc files for time-synchronized playback.

Library Organization

Organize your collection with playlists and playlist folders. Import and export .m3u8 playlists. Build and maintain your music library exactly how you want it.

Customization

Extensive customization options let you personalize the interface to match your preferences and listening habits.

Supporter Features

Support development with an optional donation or unlock the Supporter tier for a badge and additional app icons. All core playback features are completely free.

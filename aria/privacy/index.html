Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Overview

Aria is designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Aria does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. There are no analytics, tracking, or advertisements. All music playback happens entirely on your device.

Your Music Files

Aria accesses only the music files you explicitly add through the Files app or direct import. Your library remains local to your device, and your listening activity is completely private.

In-App Purchases

Optional donations and supporter features are processed through Apple's App Store. All payment information is handled securely by Apple. No payment details are accessible to the app or developer.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync for app settings and preferences, this data is stored in your personal iCloud account and protected by Apple's encryption. This data is not accessible outside your iCloud account.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at aria@michaelcheng.dev

Ultramarine

Navidrome client for iOS

Ultramarine is a native SwiftUI client for Navidrome, designed for high-fidelity streaming from your personal music server. Built on the foundation of Indigo, with a completely new networking stack and audio pipeline optimized for reliable Hi-Fi playback. No tracking, no ads, just your music.

Hi-Fi Streaming

Stream lossless audio directly from your Navidrome server with full support for FLAC, ALAC, WAV, and other high-quality formats. The new audio pipeline ensures stable playback and consistent sound quality across your entire library.

Navidrome Integration

Connect directly to your Navidrome instance and access your complete music collection instantly. Browse artists, albums, playlists, and genres with fast loading and efficient background syncing.

Metadata & Lyrics

Full metadata support displays album art, title, artist, album, and detailed track information streamed from your server. Embedded lyrics and synced .lrc files are supported for time-synchronized playback.

Library Experience

Navigate large collections smoothly with a clean, responsive interface. Ultramarine is designed for fast browsing, quick search, and seamless playback across extensive self-hosted music libraries.

Built on Indigo

Ultramarine reuses the design philosophy of Indigo while introducing a completely new networking architecture and streaming-focused playback engine, purpose-built for remote libraries and high-quality audio delivery.
